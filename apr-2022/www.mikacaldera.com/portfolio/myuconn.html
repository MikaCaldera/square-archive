MyUConn

MyUConn is a web application for the University of Connecticut. It encompasses many aspects of the University, including transportation, dining, activities, events, etc. In reality, it is one application that consists of many other applications, radios, maps, etc. In creating each app there was a long thought process, as I considered how to help the audience navigate the information they would be interested in, and how to make it easy to use. First, I worked on a dry erase board, creating little thumbnail mockups of the app, then I moved to wireframes. Afterward, I moved into actual design.
